About Mahasathram

A sacred gathering of devotion, discourse and community

The Akhila Bharatha Srimad Bhagavatha Maha Sathram is one of the largest annual Vaishnavite spiritual retreats in South India. Primarily hosted in Kerala, these 10-to-11-day mega-events feature continuous recitations of the 18,000 verses of the Srimad Bhagavatam, mass discourses by prominent scholars, and free daily food for all attendees.
